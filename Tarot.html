Tarot Readings at Anathema

We offer Tarot readings as a quiet place to pause and listen. Each session is held with care, respect, and honesty a conversation between cards, reader, and seeker.

Tarot isn’t about prediction here, but reflection: a way to sit with what’s unfolding, name what’s been stirring, and find clarity where things feel tangled.

Below, you’ll find our Tarot readers, each with their own voice, rhythm, and way of working with the cards. Take your time, read through, and trust what draws you. The right reader tends to find you when you’re ready.
